About me

This is me.

I combine scientific rigour, international practice and a clear understanding of how change actually works in organisations.

PhD / MBA / MSc Teaching since 2006 25+ years international practice AI governance, training, keynotes

For many years I have supported organisations in demanding roles: as interim manager, programme and project lead, consultant, coach and sparring partner in SMEs, corporations and regulated environments.

I am not just interested in strategies on slides, but in structures, processes and decisions that become sustainable in everyday operations and give people orientation.

With Dr. DirKInstitute I have created a space where AI competence is conveyed in a practical way – through training, workshops, keynotes and governance consulting based on genuine understanding and operational experience.

Credentials

  • Lecturer in ITSM, ISO 27001, ISO 20000, ISO 42001, GDPR, KRITIS, NIS-2 and AI Governance
  • Bridging science, teaching and operational implementation
  • Long-standing practice in international matrix organisations and transformation programmes
  • Systemic perspective on responsibility, evidence and real-world feasibility

What I stand for

  • Clear language instead of buzzwords and consulting fog
  • End-to-end thinking from analysis to anchoring
  • Open, transparent and respectful collaboration
  • Feasible solutions with a focus on people, impact and responsibility
