Why now

AI is transforming business – those who don't steer will be steered.

01

AI competence is missing at decision-maker level

Board members and executives need to assess AI risks, recognise opportunities and make informed decisions – without being developers themselves.

02

The EU AI Act demands action

AI Literacy under Article 4, risk classification, documentation obligations – the new requirements affect every company that uses AI.

03

Theory alone is not enough

Real AI competence comes from understanding, applying and reflecting – in workshops, training and concrete use cases from your own organisation.
